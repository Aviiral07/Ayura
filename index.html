‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Find quick answers about our verification process and services.

How does the Doctor Verification System work? +

Our system cross-references the doctor's name, registration number, and state with official public databases (simulated here). A match confirms the practitioner is registered and in good standing.

Is the appointment booking service secure? +

Yes, all appointment data is encrypted and securely transmitted. We only facilitate booking with practitioners whose credentials we have verified.

What should I do if a doctor is "Not Verified"? +

If a doctor appears "Not Verified," it means we couldn't find a direct match. You should verify their credentials with the local State Medical Council or report them via our "Report Fake Doctor" section if you suspect fraud.

Can the AI Chatbot diagnose my condition? +

No. The AI Chatbot is for informational purposes only. It can provide general health information and preliminary guidance, but it is not a substitute for a professional medical diagnosis or emergency care.

Is my personal information kept confidential? +

Absolutely. We adhere strictly to our Privacy Policy. All information, especially contact details submitted for reporting or appointments, is handled with the utmost confidentiality.
